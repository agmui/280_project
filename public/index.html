WHAT IS ROBOMASTERS?

Robomaster is a robotics cometition started by DJI that has millions of viewers worldwide. Each year, teams design and build robots which fire either 17mm (Paintball size) or 42mm (Golfball size) projectiles at other robots in an arena. The ultimate objective of this being to destroy the opposing teams base, by hitting it's force sensitive armor panels with your projectiles.

WE LOVE TO LEARN!

The Rose-Hulman Thornbots are dedicated to an accessible and educational environment. We have no application process or requirements. With an emphasis on mentorship and collaboration, all a new member needs is an interest in the team and a passion for robotics.
